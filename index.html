What We Do

We create, buy and sell digital pieces of art which are non fungible in nature (NFTs)

Areas of our core focus :

Non Fungibility

Every piece across are collections shares one commonality and that is all of our artworks are rare in nature. We only sell pieces that are rare and hard to replicate

Quality

The other commonality of our artworks across all our collections is that all of them are of top-notch quality, we pay special attention to details and colours and only after thorough analysis we put it for listing only if it has passed all our quality tests
